Capital High Boys Golf 2022 State Champions

For the first time in 35 years, the Capital High School Boys Golf team brought home the state title.

This honor earned the team an invitation to the prestigious PGA National High School Tournament at PGA Headquarters in Frisco, Texas July 17-19, 2023.

Our team will need to raise the funds necessary to compete in Frisco and represent both Montana and our hometown. We would be so grateful and honored to count on you to help us make this once-in-a- lifetime experience a reality!
